Our Story

Stock Pulse was born from a simple frustration: millions of young Indians want to invest, but most platforms feel too complex and intimidating. Existing brokers serve experienced traders well — but first-timers are left behind.

We built Stock Pulse as a safe practice ground — trade with virtual money, learn by doing, understand P&L in real time, and let AI guide your decisions. No real money at risk, no fear of mistakes.

Today Stock Pulse serves traders across 650+ Indian cities, with 60% of users being first-generation investors from Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities.
