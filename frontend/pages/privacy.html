Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register for Stock Pulse, we collect:

  • Personal details: name, email address, mobile number
  • Profile data: city, risk profile, investment preferences
  • Usage data: pages visited, trades simulated, features used
  • Device data: browser type, IP address, time zone

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Stock Pulse simulation platform
  • Personalise AI recommendations and dashboard experience
  • Send market alerts, newsletters (only if opted in)
  • Improve platform features based on usage patterns
  • Comply with applicable Indian laws and regulations

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including JWT authentication, BCrypt password hashing (strength 12), and HTTPS-only communication. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

Stock Pulse is a simulation platform — no real money or financial data is ever processed or stored.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share anonymised, aggregated data for research purposes only.

5. Cookies

We use minimal session cookies to maintain your login state. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Export your account data

To exercise any right, email us at privacy@stockpulse.in

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@stockpulse.in
