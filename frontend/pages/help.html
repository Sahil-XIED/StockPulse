Frequently Asked Questions
How do I buy a stock on Stock Pulse?▼

Go to the Trade section from the sidebar. Select your stock, enter quantity, choose order type (Market or Limit), review the total, then click Place Buy Order. Funds are deducted from your virtual balance instantly.

How is my P&L calculated?▼

P&L = (Current Market Price − Average Buy Price) × Quantity. If you bought 10 shares of RELIANCE at ’2,640 and the current price is ’2,847, your unrealised P&L = ’2,070 (+7.8%). The Portfolio section shows this in real time.

What is Stock Pulse? Is it real trading?▼

Stock Pulse is a simulation platform for education only. No real money is involved. All prices are simulated and all trades are virtual. It is designed to help you learn investing risk-free before using a real broker.

Why are prices not updating?▼

Prices update every 5 seconds. If they appear frozen: (1) Check your internet connection, (2) Ensure the Spring Boot backend is running at localhost:8080, (3) Hard refresh the page with Ctrl+Shift+R.

How do I reset my virtual balance?▼

Contact our support team at support@stockpulse.in and we will reset your balance to ’1,00,000. A self-serve reset feature is coming soon in the Profile settings.

How do I add stocks to my watchlist?▼

Go to the Watchlist section and click the + Add Stock button. Search for any NSE-listed stock symbol (e.g. RELIANCE, TCS, HDFC) and click Add. Your watchlist is saved to the database and persists across sessions.
