Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Stock Pulse

How do I create a Stock Pulse account?▼

Click Sign Up on the login page. Fill in your name, email, mobile number, select a risk profile (Conservative, Moderate, or Aggressive), and create a password of at least 8 characters. Your account starts with ’1,00,000 virtual balance.

How do I reset my password?▼

Click Forgot password on the login page and enter your registered email. A reset link will arrive within 2 minutes. The link is valid for 30 minutes. Check your spam folder if you do not see it.

What is the difference between Market and Limit orders?▼

A Market Order executes immediately at the current price. A Limit Order executes only when the stock reaches your specified price. Use Limit Orders when you want to buy at a specific target price rather than the current market rate.

Why am I getting an Insufficient balance error?▼

Your available balance is less than the order total. Try reducing the quantity, choosing a lower-priced stock, or selling existing holdings to free up balance. Your current balance is shown in the green pill in the top navigation bar.

How is average buy price calculated?▼

Average price = Total amount invested ÷ Total shares held. Example: Buy 10 shares at ’100 + 10 shares at ’120. Average = (1000 + 1200) ÷ 20 = ’110. This updates automatically each time you buy the same stock.

What does unrealised P&L mean?▼

Unrealised P&L is the profit or loss on shares you currently hold but have not yet sold. It becomes realised only when you sell. Formula: (Current Price − Average Buy Price) × Quantity.

Prices are not updating. What should I do?▼

Stock Pulse updates prices every 5 seconds. If prices appear frozen: (1) Check your internet connection, (2) Ensure the Spring Boot backend is running at localhost:8080, (3) Hard refresh with Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R.

Is Stock Pulse real trading? Am I using real money?▼

No. Stock Pulse is 100% a simulation platform for education only. No real money is involved at any stage. All prices are simulated by our scheduler and all trades are completely virtual. No real brokerage account is created.
